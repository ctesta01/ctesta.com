Attack America’s overlapping miseries: Why going big on relief is an economic, public health and moral imperative

This opinion piece we (Nancy Krieger, Christian Testa, Pamela Waterman and Jarvis Chen) published in the New York Daily News presents a data driven perspective on the concurrent tragedies of economic insecurity and COVID-19. We argue that federal relief addressing both of these needs is urgently necessary.
